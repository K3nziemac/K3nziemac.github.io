Audience

Our Audience would be anyone who has a desire to know about their upcoming weather ahead of time. Typically people who are old enough to have an electronic device and a desire to learn more.

Personas

ASHLEY- Ashley is a 24 year old studying nursing. She has a long commute everyday to school so she liked to check the weather ahead of time to see how it will affect her arrival time to school.

NATHAN- Nathan is a swim teacher and depends on this weather app to tell him the weather. He needs it to see if any of the bad weather will affect the pool so he can let his students know ahead of time.

5 scenarios

  • People who are just curious about the weather
  • People who use it in their planning
  • People who surf so they can look at the tide
  • People who need to know how to dress
  • People who need to know whether or not to prepare for bad weather coming
